About Me

I work in coastal environmental restoriation at Pontchatrain Conservancy. There, I am the Coastal DataSharing Program Developer and Technology Coordinator. Part of my job requires handling data requests as they come in and packaging different data sets (either the raw salinity data or interpolated salinity contours) for the end user. Folks who use the data are primarily other non profits, government agencies, and academic institutions. Sometimes we get requests from commercial and recreational fisher-folk who are trying to get a sense of the salinity and where the fish might be biting.

Another aspect of my job is to assist different departments with their technology needs. This might mean updating a webpage (using wordpress, and maybe soon actual HTML/CSS and javascript in the near future!) or the gift shop, creating maps for tree identification, or operating a drone for restoration monitoring.

Recently, I've been helping the education department with a marine debris project, specifically the data collection, interpretation,and visualization. You can learn more about this project here

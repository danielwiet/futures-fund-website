Welcome to my website. If you are new here, please take a look around. You might just find something interesting about me.

This website was created as a capstone project for Coding Bootcamp (Coding for the Web) Level 1, 2, and 3 provided by The Futures Fund. If you haven't checked them out, I highly encourage you to do so. They can be found here.

I enrolled in Coding Bootcamp (Coding for the Web) to gain experience in web building. I have a little experience in Python and thought that it would be good to round it out with HTML, CSS, and javascript. At times, it hasn't been easy, but most rewarding things in life come with a little challenge.

I've gained a lot from this process and I am excited to see what else I can do with the knowledge accuired in the past few months. This site is by no means complete, and I will continue to add content to the site as time permits.
